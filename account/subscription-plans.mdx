## Free Trial

All new Pro subscriptions include a **7-day free trial** period:

- Full access to Pro features during trial
- No credit card required upfront for trial start
- Automatic conversion to paid subscription after trial ends
- Cancel anytime during trial with no charges

## Subscription Management

### Upgrading to Pro

To upgrade your account to Pro:

1. Navigate to your account settings
2. Select "Upgrade to Pro"
3. Choose monthly or annual billing
4. Complete the checkout process
5. Your trial begins immediately

### Managing Your Subscription

You can manage your subscription through the Stripe billing portal:

- Update payment methods
- View invoice history
- Change billing frequency
- Cancel subscription

<Note>
The billing portal provides secure access to all your subscription and payment information.
</Note>

## Subscription Lifecycle

### Active Subscription

When your subscription is active:

- **Status**: `active`
- Full access to Pro features
- Automatic renewal at end of billing period
- Billing period start and end dates tracked

### Trial Period

During your free trial:

- **Status**: `trialing` or `active`
- Trial start and end dates recorded
- Full Pro feature access
- Converts to paid subscription when trial ends

### Cancellation

When you cancel a subscription:

- Access continues until end of billing period
- No automatic renewal
- `cancelAtPeriodEnd` flag set to `true`
- Cancel date tracked for reference

<Warning>
Cancelled subscriptions remain active until the end of your paid period. You won't be charged for subsequent periods.
</Warning>

## Billing Details

### Payment Processing

Conar uses Stripe for secure payment processing:

- Industry-standard security
- PCI DSS compliant
- Support for major credit cards
- Automatic invoice generation

### Invoicing

You'll receive invoices:

- At the start of each billing period
- After trial period ends
- Via email to your registered address
- Accessible through the billing portal

### Subscription Status

Your subscription can have the following statuses:

- `active` - Subscription is active and in good standing
- `trialing` - Currently in free trial period
- `incomplete` - Payment information needed
- `past_due` - Payment failed, needs attention
- `canceled` - Subscription has been cancelled
- `unpaid` - Payment attempts failed

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?">
    Yes, you can change your billing frequency through the billing portal. Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when my trial ends?">
    Your subscription automatically converts to a paid subscription using your chosen billing frequency. You'll be charged at the end of the trial period.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription anytime?">
    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I access my invoices?">
    All invoices are available through the Stripe billing portal, accessible from your account settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
